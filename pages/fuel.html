Fuel Tank
Flat, irritable, scattered, or emotionally reactive. This is depletion — not character.
Your self-regulation tank is smaller than average and burns faster. When it's empty, executive function goes offline. Not a flaw — a fuel problem.

What Drained You?

60-Second S.T.O.P. Reset

  • SStop. Pause whatever you're doing. Completely. Break the cycle.
  • TTake a breath. One slow inhale through nose, exhale through mouth. Activates parasympathetic nervous system.
  • OObserve. What's happening in your body? Tight chest? Scattered thoughts? Heavy limbs? Name it.
  • PProceed. One intentional next action. Not autopilot.

Mid-Day Energy Reset (15–20 min)

  • 01Go outside. Sunlight and fresh air are literal brain resets.
  • 02Move your body. Walk, stretch, anything physical. Changes brain chemistry directly.
  • 03No phone during this time. Let the mind rest from inputs.
  • 04Come back and identify your one most important next action.

Full Depletion

Don't make decisions. Decisions from a depleted state are almost always wrong.

  • 01Move your body for 20 minutes. Non-negotiable.
  • 02Talk to one person you trust. Verbal processing resets the nervous system.
  • 03Return to your anchors: faith, freedom, family, fitness.
  • 04Do one tiny creative thing. Anything. Creation produces dopamine and breaks the cycle.
