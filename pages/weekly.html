Weekly Recalibration
7-day sweep. Kill drift. Reset priorities. Run top to bottom.
1. North Star: What am I pretending not to know?
2. Top 1–2 Outcomes This Week
3. Cost Audit: What quietly taxed me last week?
4. Subtraction List: What gets removed to protect the outcomes?
5. Avoidance Audit: What conversation/action must happen within 72 hours?
6. System Move: What gets standardized so willpower isn't required?
7. Never Miss Twice: What slipped once? How do I prevent the second miss?
Gate: If the week has priorities but no subtraction → you're lying.
