5-Day Just Move Intervention

When identity lag is the bottleneck — not strategy, not information.

  • Day 1Force irreversible decisions. Pick one direction. Publicly commit. Burn the escape hatches. The plan is already good enough. Your intelligence has become friction.
  • Day 2Cap thinking. Mandate output. One ship per day. No polishing past usefulness. "If you say it's not ready, they'd ask: Ready for what?"
  • Day 3Collapse feedback cycles. Ship → observe → adjust within 24–72 hours. No long planning sessions. You don't lack information. You lack consequences fast enough to force motion.
  • Day 4Ban fake productivity. No reformatting. No rewording. Only actions that create exposure, create commitment, or create irreversible progress.
  • Day 5Redefine "good" as "done." Momentum forgives imperfection. You act first. You interpret later. No waiting to feel ready.
