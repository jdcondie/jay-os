State Work
Manifestation Process
Use when state is low, motivation is absent, or you need to reconnect to the vision before a big move.

The 3-Step Process

  • 01Decide: Make a clear, unwavering decision about the desired outcome. Stop asking "how" — just say yes to the vision. When you decide: thoughts align, perception shifts, reality begins to reshape. Focus on what you want, not what you don't want.
  • 02Non-Resistance: Downregulate your nervous system. Eliminate internal conflict. Resistance (fear, anxiety, doubt) prevents miracles. Tools: breathwork, affirmations, movement, cold exposure. Ask: are you playing to win — or playing not to lose?
  • 03Embody: Start living as if your desired outcome is already reality. Don't chase the miracle — let it come to meet you. Focus on the vision, not the problem. Force kills momentum. Stay in motion. Feel it now.
Decide. Detach. Embody. That's how you co-create with God, energy, the universe — whatever you believe in. You don't chase miracles. You align with them.

The 6-Step Check-In

Run this when you need to reconnect to the vision. Don't rush it.

  • 1Energetic Check-In: When was a time you knew something good was going to happen — and it did? Which version of you were you embodying? What space am I in right now? Do I feel empowered and pulled forward?
  • 2Detangle Dense Energy: Do you feel heavy or light? Distracted or present? Say: "My ___ is no longer a threat." Then: "It is safe to ___."
  • 3Neutralize Distractions: Return to presence. One breath. Notice what's here right now.
  • 4Self-Love Check-In: Do you believe you deserve it? Can you look in the mirror and speak to yourself with love? Do you truly believe in yourself — without a doubt?
  • 5Identify True Motive: Embrace the desire with intentional energy. Why does this actually matter to you at the deepest level?
  • 6Visualize It On Its Way: Feel it happening in this moment. It should feel effortless, not forced. No hesitation. Take the next step immediately. Hold this state as often and as long as possible. Give thanks.
