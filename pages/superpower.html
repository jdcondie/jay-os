Cognitive Trap
Stability disguised as safety

Safety is usually stability wearing a mask. Titles that don't produce the life become dead weight. Approval disguised as ambition.

Energy Trap
Environments that reward optics

Environments that reward optics over outcomes. Overcommitting to other people's urgency. Performative process instead of real leverage.

Execution Trap
Clarity in the head doesn't count as done

Solving a problem mentally kills the drive to ship it. The loop closes too early. Solved is not the same as shipped.

Structure Trap
Forced sequencing creates friction

Rigid SOPs override instinct and drain energy fast. Structure earned in motion works. Structure imposed in advance is a cage.
