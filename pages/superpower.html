Cognitive Trap
Stability disguised as safety

Titles that don't produce the life become dead weight. Approval can masquerade as ambition. The constraint looks comfortable from the outside.

Energy Trap
Environments that reward optics

Overcommitting to others' urgency. Performative process over real output. The stack drains when the room measures appearances over outcomes.

Execution Trap
Clarity in the head doesn't count as done

Mentally solving a problem kills the drive to ship it. The loop closes too early. Solved ≠ shipped.

Structure Trap
Forced sequencing creates friction

Rigid SOPs override instinct and drain energy fast. Structure earned in motion. Structure imposed in advance is a cage.
