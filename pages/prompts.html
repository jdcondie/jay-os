Clarity Questions (12 Filters)

1
Does this decision move me closer to the life I want — or just delay discomfort? (Freedom > Avoidance)
2
If I already trusted that success was inevitable, what choice would I make? (Faith > Fear)
3
Am I choosing based on who I've been — or who I'm becoming? (Identity > History)
4
Will this decision give me more time, energy, or creativity — or take it away? (Energy is currency. Protect it.)
5
Does this require me to shrink, contort, or tolerate what I've already outgrown?
6
Is this coming from alignment — or anxiety?
7
If I said yes to this, would it support my freedom to travel, move, create, and be with family? (Time + Location Freedom = Non-Negotiable)
8
Does this allow me to control my schedule and work from joy, not obligation? (Agency > Approval)
9
Would the version of me living in the mountains, traveling in winter, and building creative projects say yes to this? (Zoom out to your 2-year vision)
10
Does this help me give to others, lift others, or teach something that frees someone else? (Impact = Fuel)
11
Would I be proud to tell my future kids I chose this path — and why? (Legacy framing)
12
If I knew I couldn't fail, is this what I'd choose? (Let truth speak louder than fear)
