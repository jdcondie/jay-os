Installation
Behavior Rewiring
7-step protocol for installing a new automatic behavior. Run one target at a time. 14–30 days minimum.

The Core Rule

New behavior becomes automatic when identity changes first. "I am the kind of person who ___" is more powerful than any habit stack.

The 7 Steps

Step 1 — Target Acquisition: Precise arrival condition (observable, specific)
Step 2 — Future A (Desired): Normal day when I've arrived — what do I see/hear/feel?
Step 2 — Future B (Failure): If nothing changes for 6–12 months — what's the cost?
Step 3 — Identity Statement: "I am the kind of person who..."
Step 3 — 3 Daily Behaviors this identity defaults to
Step 4 — Top 3 cues that trigger the old script
Step 4 — 3 environment changes in the next 24 hours
Step 5 — Who expects the new version of me to show up? (names)
Step 5 — Emotional tag for the NEW behavior (pride/safety/relief/love)
Step 5 — Emotional tag for the OLD path (cost/decline/regret)
Step 6 — "If I miss once, I do this next" rule
Step 7 — Identity reinforcement line (short, repeatable)
