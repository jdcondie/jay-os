Toolbox

Decide (1–3 min): State/write bold outcome; place in view.
Release (2 min): Name tension, deep breath, reset posture.
Embody (2–5 min): Visualize outcome, move accordingly.
Ship Rule: Draft → refine → ship after second pass.
Idea Capture (1 min/idea): Dump to inbox, tag later.
Expansion Check: Does this feel exciting or heavy? If heavy, adjust scope until it feels exciting.
