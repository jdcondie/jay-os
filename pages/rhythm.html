Morning (First 2 Hours)

90-Second Morning Calibration

  • 0–30sState Lock: Stand. Exhale fully. Eyes up. Jaw relaxed. Say once: "I decide fast. I move clean. I finish."
  • 30–60sOrientation: What must be true by tonight? What gets ignored without guilt? What protects momentum? One phrase each.
  • 60–90sDominant Move: Single action that makes everything else easier. If done = day is a win. Schedule it first. Guard it.
